FIFA 23





FIFA 23 is a football video game published by Electronic Arts (or simply EA). It is the 30th installment in the FIFA series that is developed by EA Sports, and the final installment under the FIFA banner, and released worldwide on 30 September 2022 for Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Windows, Xbox One and Xbox Series X/S.
